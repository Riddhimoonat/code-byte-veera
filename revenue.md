# 💰 Revenue Model — Veera

> "Building a scalable safety ecosystem with sustainable monetization."

---

## 📌 Overview

**Veera** is a personal safety platform offering:
- SOS alerting  
- Real-time tracking  
- ML-based risk detection  

Our monetization strategy follows:

> **Grow First → Monetize Smartly → Scale with B2B & AI**

---

## 🚀 1. User Acquisition Strategy

### 📱 Social Media Growth
- Promote app via Instagram ads  
- Use:
  - Reels & short-form content  
  - Influencer collaborations  
  - Safety awareness campaigns  

**Goal:**
- Rapid user acquisition  
- Build trust before monetization  

---

## 🟢 2. Freemium Model (Core Revenue)

### 🆓 Free Features
- SOS emergency alerts  
- Emergency contacts  
- Basic risk detection  

**Purpose:**  
Ensure accessibility and mass adoption  

---

### 💎 Premium Features (Paid)

- Advanced AI risk predictions  
- Continuous real-time tracking  
- Smart alerts before danger  
- Auto SOS trigger (future)  
- Location history  

**Pricing:**
- ₹49–₹99/month (avg ₹79)

---

## 🟡 3. In-App Advertisements

- Non-intrusive ads (banners/rewarded)  
- No ads during emergencies  

**Revenue Source:**
- Ad networks (e.g., AdMob)

---

## 🔵 4. B2B / Institutional Revenue (Primary Driver)

### 🏫 Target Customers
- Colleges  
- Corporates  
- Police departments  

### Features:
- Live SOS dashboard  
- Risk heatmaps  
- Analytics  

**Pricing:**
- ₹10,000–₹50,000/month  

> B2B partnerships form the **largest and most stable revenue stream**

---

## 🟣 5. Future Revenue Streams

- Auto SOS (AI-based emergency detection)  
- ML Risk API (for external apps like ride-sharing)  
- Safety analytics (government/NGOs)  
- Emergency service partnerships  

---

## 💡 Why Users Will Pay

- Safety is a **high-priority need**, especially for women and students  
- Real-time risk intelligence provides **preventive protection**  
- Families are willing to pay for **tracking and emergency alerts**  
- Premium features offer **peace of mind, not just functionality**
